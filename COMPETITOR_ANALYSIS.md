# Hunt-X Competitor Analysis

## Market Overview
AI-powered job search tools are growing rapidly. Market size: $500M+, growing 25% YoY.

## Direct Competitors

### 1. Teal (tealhq.com)
**What they do:**
- Resume builder with AI optimization
- Job application tracker
- Chrome extension for job boards
- Interview prep

**Pricing:**
- Free tier (limited)
- Premium: $9/week or $29/month

**Strengths:**
- Beautiful UI/UX
- Strong brand recognition
- Chrome extension integration
- Large user base

**Weaknesses:**
- Subscription fatigue ($29/month)
- Limited AI customization
- US-focused job boards

**Hunt-X Differentiation:**
- ✅ One-time €49 option (no subscription fatigue)
- ✅ More AI customization per job
- ✅ Global market focus

## Market Gaps Hunt-X Can Fill

1. **European/Global market** — Most competitors are US-focused

3. **Quality over quantity** — Not auto-apply spam
